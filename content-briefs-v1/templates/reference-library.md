## INTRO FORMAT

**Total:** 100-150 words

**Structure:**
1. Opening (40-50 words)
   - Sentence 1: Direct answer with winners
   - Sentence 2: Authority statement
2. Disclosure (50-75 words)

**Forbidden:**
- ❌ "Welcome to..."
- ❌ "Looking for..."
- ❌ Rhetorical questions

## DATING LANGUAGE RULES

### NEVER in Title/H1
❌ "October 2025"
❌ "Review 2025"
❌ "Best Sites 2025"

### USE Instead
✅ "Comprehensive Review"
✅ "The #1 Rated App"
✅ "Last Updated" badge after H1

### WHERE Dates OK
- ✅ "Last Updated" badge
- ✅ Schema dateModified
- ✅ T&Cs verification date
- ✅ Within content: "As of October 2025..."
