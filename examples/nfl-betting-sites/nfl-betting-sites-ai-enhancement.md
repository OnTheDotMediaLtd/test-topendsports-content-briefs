          <li>49ers to beat Seattle (-110 odds)</li>
          <li>Cowboys to beat Washington (-150 odds)</li>

        <p><strong>Why Use Parlays?</strong> Larger potential payouts from small bets, but higher risk since you need all legs to win.</p>

      <li><strong>Track Spending:</strong> Keep records of what you bet and how much you win/lose</li>
      <li><strong>Never Chase Losses:</strong> Accept losses and don't try to win it back immediately</li>
      <li><strong>Entertainment Only:</strong> View betting as entertainment, not a way to make money</li>
      <li><strong>Take Breaks:</strong> If you're losing or feeling stressed, step away</li>
    </ul>

    <p>All major sportsbooks offer responsible gambling tools:</p>

      <li><strong>Deposit Limits:</strong> Set daily/weekly/monthly spending caps</li>
      <li><strong>Loss Limits:</strong> Limit how much you can lose in a time period</li>
      <li><strong>Betting Limits:</strong> Cap maximum bet amounts</li>
      <li><strong>Self-Exclusion:</strong> Voluntarily exclude yourself from betting (temporary or permanent)</li>
      <li><strong>Reality Checks:</strong> Reminders of how long you've been betting</li>
    </ul>
