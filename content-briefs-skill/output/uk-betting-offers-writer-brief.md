### FAQ #7: "What wagering requirements apply to welcome offers?" ← **"welcome offers betting"** (350/mo)

**Answer Focus:**
- Wagering requirement = how many times you must bet the bonus before withdrawal
- Common requirements:
  - 1x wagering (bet bonus amount once) - best
  - 3x wagering (bet three times) - common
  - 5x+ wagering (bet five times+) - avoid
- Minimum odds requirements (usually 1.50-2.00)
- Restricted markets (e.g., covering all outcomes doesn't count)
- Expiry timeframe (7-30 days typical)
- Example calculation: £20 bonus at 3x wagering = £60 in bets needed
- Lower wagering = better value
- Check our T&Cs section for each operator's requirements
