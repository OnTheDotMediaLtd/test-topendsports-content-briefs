## OPTIMIZED FAQs (8)

1. **"How many groups are in the 2026 World Cup?"** ← "2026 world cup groups" (1,900/mo)
   - 12 groups (A through L) with 4 teams each, expanded from 8 groups in previous tournaments due to the increase from 32 to 48 teams, top 2 from each group advance plus 8 best 3rd-place teams

2. **"Can I bet on World Cup group winners?"** ← "world cup group winner odds" (10/mo)
   - Yes, all major US sportsbooks offer group winner markets, bet on which team finishes top, FanDuel/BetMGM/DraftKings/bet365/Caesars all carry group winner odds, separate from outright tournament winner futures

3. **"What is the World Cup group of death?"** ← "world cup group of death" (350/mo)
   - The toughest group based on team rankings and perceived quality, typically features 3+ strong teams competing for 2 guaranteed spots, offers tighter odds and more value on draws and upsets for bettors

4. **"When was the World Cup 2026 draw?"** ← "world cup draw" (60,000/mo)
   - December 13, 2025 in Zurich, Switzerland, 48 teams placed into 12 groups using pot system based on FIFA rankings, host nations USA/Mexico/Canada placed in separate groups as top seeds, group betting odds available within hours

5. **"How does World Cup group stage betting work?"** ← "world cup group stage betting" (10/mo)
   - Includes group winner markets, individual match betting, and prop bets, match betting covers moneyline (1X2), Asian handicap spreads, and over/under goals totals, can also bet on group stage props like total goals in a group

6. **"What are the best bets for World Cup group stage?"** ← "world cup group betting odds" (10/mo)
   - Group winner bets on heavy favorites for low-risk accumulators, draw bets on 3rd matchday games where both teams have qualified, under 2.5 goals in opening matches, cross-group parlays combining multiple group winners

7. **"Where can I find World Cup group predictions?"** ← "world cup group predictions" (100/mo)
   - Available on major sportsbooks including FanDuel, BetMGM, and bet365, check group winner odds for implied probability of each team finishing first, this page includes per-group breakdowns with analysis of favorites and value picks

8. **"How are World Cup group standings determined?"** ← "world cup group standings" (3,400/mo)
   - 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss, tiebreakers: goal difference, goals scored, head-to-head record, then fair play points, top 2 advance per group, in 2026 the 8 best 3rd-place teams also advance
