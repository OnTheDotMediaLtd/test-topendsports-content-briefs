## APPENDIX: UK FREE BET TERMINOLOGY

**For Writer Reference (USA vs. UK Terms)**

| UK Term | USA Equivalent | Definition |
|---------|----------------|------------|
| Free Bet | Risk-Free Bet | Bet placed without using own funds (stake not returned) |
| Accumulator (Acca) | Parlay | Multiple selections combined, all must win |
| Each-Way | Win/Place | Bet split into win and place components |
| Treble | 3-Team Parlay | Accumulator with exactly 3 selections |
| Odds (Fractional) | Odds (Decimal/American) | 2/1 (UK) = 3.0 (Decimal) = +200 (USA) |
| Bookmaker | Sportsbook | Betting operator |
| Punter | Bettor | Person placing bets |
| Stake | Wager | Amount bet |
| Returns | Payout | Total returned (stake + profit) |
| Wagering Requirement | Rollover/Playthrough | How many times bonus must be bet |
