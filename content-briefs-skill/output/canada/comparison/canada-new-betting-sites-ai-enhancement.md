- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ in Canada; 18+ in Alberta, Manitoba, Quebec; ID verification mandatory

- **Welcome Offer:** Tiered bonuses from $250-$750 based on deposit level; max $750 on $1,000+ deposit
- **Wagering:** 25x playthrough; valid for 45 days; VIP members receive extended validity
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ in Canada; 18+ in AB/MB/QC; documents required for account verification
- **Restrictions:** Bonus not applicable to existing customers; applies only to first deposit

### Wyns T&Cs {#wyn-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** Up to $300 bonus on first two deposits; split equally between deposits
- **Wagering:** 20x playthrough on bonus; expires 60 days from activation
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ throughout Canada; 18+ in AB, MB, QC; proof of residence required
- **Restrictions:** Free bets cannot be combined; minimum odds 1.5 for bonus bets

### Lucky7even T&Cs {#l7e-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** $100-$400 based on deposit amount; lottery-style prize multiplier feature included
- **Wagering:** 35x playthrough; 30-day deadline; lottery winnings separate from bonus
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ in Canada (18+ in select provinces); monthly spend limits available
- **Restrictions:** Restricted games list applies to bonus funds; progressive jackpots excluded

### Festival Play T&Cs {#fst-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** Up to $600 bonus + 50 free bets on event-specific markets
- **Wagering:** 25x on bonus amount; free bets have 5x playthrough; valid 90 days
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ nationally; 18+ in AB/MB/QC; festival event bettors prioritized
- **Restrictions:** Free bets limited to specified event markets; non-transferable

### Lizaro T&Cs {#lzr-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** $50-$200 crypto bonus or 50% match on first BTC/ETH deposit
- **Wagering:** 15x playthrough on crypto deposits; 20x on fiat equivalents; 7-day expiry
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ Canada; 18+ AB/MB/QC; crypto wallet verification required
- **Restrictions:** Stablecoin deposits only; withdrawal limits apply for new accounts

### Legendplay T&Cs {#lgd-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** Up to $350 bonus + esports-specific tournament entry tokens
- **Wagering:** 30x playthrough; 45-day validity; esports tokens have separate 10x requirement
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ Canada; 18+ AB/MB/QC; dual-account prohibition (esports/sports)
- **Restrictions:** Esports tokens cannot be withdrawn as cash; minimum 2.0 odds per bet

### Funbet T&Cs {#fun-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** $200-$500 based on social referral level; bonus increases with friend invites
- **Wagering:** 25x playthrough; social sharers receive 1.5x bonus multiplier; valid 60 days
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ Canada; 18+ AB/MB/QC; social profile required for verification
- **Restrictions:** Referral bonus capped at 5 invitations per week; bonuses not stackable

### DirectionBet T&Cs {#dir-tcs}

- **Welcome Offer:** $150-$400 AI-powered bonus recommendation; personalized wagering structure
- **Wagering:** AI-adjusted playthrough (15-35x based on user profile); 60-day activation window
- **Age & Eligibility:** 19+ Canada; 18+ AB/MB/QC; AI profile analysis requires email verification
- **Restrictions:** AI recommendations advisory only; responsible gambling limits apply; account review required annually
