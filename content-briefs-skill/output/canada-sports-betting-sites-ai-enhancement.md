## RESPONSIBLE GAMBLING & SUPPORT

### Canada-Wide Helplines

**ConnexOntario: 1-866-531-2600** (Primary helpline, 24/7, toll-free, confidential)
- Immediate support for problem gambling
- Free counseling and resource access
- Referrals to treatment programs
- Self-exclusion registry (Ontario Voluntary Self-Exclusion Program)

**National Problem Gambling Helpline:** 1-800-522-4700 (General Canada reference)

### Provincial Resources

| Province | Helpline | Program |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Ontario** | 1-866-531-2600 | ConnexOntario, Voluntary Self-Exclusion |
| **Alberta** | AGLC support lines | Alberta Gaming Research Institute |
| **Quebec** | Espacejeux services | Problem gambling counseling |
| **BC** | BCLC helpline | BC Responsible Gambling Council |
| **Manitoba** | MLL oversight | Manitoba Gambling Helpline |

### Tools Available at Every Sportsbook

1. **Deposit Limits** - Daily, weekly, monthly caps enforced in real-time
2. **Session Limits** - Auto-logout after set time (e.g., 4 hours)
3. **Cooling-Off Periods** - Pause betting (1 week to 1 month)
4. **Reality Checks** - Reminder alerts every 30 minutes of session
5. **Self-Exclusion** - Voluntary ban (6 months to permanent) across some provincial registries
6. **Account Closure** - Permanent account termination
7. **Reality Checks** - Time spent and money wagered displays

### Signs of Problem Gambling

- Betting more than intended
- Chasing losses with bigger bets
- Hiding gambling from family/friends
- Gambling with bill money or savings
- Feeling anxious without betting access
- Failed attempts to stop or reduce

### Getting Help

If any of these apply, reach out to **ConnexOntario (1-866-531-2600)** immediately. Trained counselors can:
- Assess your situation
- Provide free counseling sessions
- Connect you to treatment programs
- Help with financial planning
- Support family members affected by problem gambling

**Remember:** Asking for help is strength. It works. You're not alone.
