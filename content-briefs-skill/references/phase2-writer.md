# Phase 2: Writer Brief Protocol

**Input:** Brief Control Sheet from Phase 1
**Output:** Writer Brief (2-3 pages)

---

## Step 1: Load Phase 1 Data

Extract from Brief Control Sheet:
- Target keyword and cluster
- Writer assignment
- Template type
- Brand selection
- Strategic direction
- Word count target

---

## Step 2: Mandatory Intro Format

**Total:** 100-150 words

**Structure:**
1. Opening (40-50 words)
   - Sentence 1: Direct answer with winners
   - Sentence 2: Authority statement
2. Disclosure (50-75 words)

**Example:**
```
The best NFL betting sites are FanDuel, DraftKings, and BetMGM. They offer 
the most competitive NFL odds and comprehensive prop markets, verified through 
extensive testing.

[Disclosure: We may earn commission...50-75 words...]
```

**Forbidden:**
- ❌ "Welcome to..."
- ❌ "Looking for..."
- ❌ Rhetorical questions
- ❌ Over 150 words

---

## Step 3: Build Keyword-Optimized Outline

### Template 1 (Review) Structure:
```

├── [Last Updated Badge]
├── Introduction (100-150w)
├── Comparison Table (3 brands)
├── Quick Answer Box
├── H2: [Brand] Overview (300-350w)
├── H2: Getting Started (300-350w)
├── H2: [Brand] Bonus: Current Welcome Offer (400-500w) ← "[brand] bonus"
│   ├── H3: Do I Need a [Brand] Promo Code? ← "[brand] promo code"
│   ├── H3: Complete Terms & Conditions
│   └── H3: Is This a Good Bonus?
├── H2: [Brand] App Review (500-600w) ← "[brand] app review"
│   └── H3: [Brand] App Download Guide ← "[brand] app download"
├── H2: [Signature Feature] (400-500w)
├── H2: Live Betting & Streaming (400-450w)
├── H2: Betting Markets (400-450w)
├── H2: Banking (400-450w)
│   └── H3: [Brand] Withdrawal Time ← "[brand] withdrawal time"
├── H2: [Brand] vs Competitors (400-500w) ← "[brand] vs [competitor]"
├── H2: Is [Brand] Legal? (300-350w) ← "is [brand] legal"
│   └── H3: Where is [Brand] Available? ← "[brand] states"
├── Pros & Cons Summary
├── FAQs (7 minimum)
├── Final Verdict (200-250w)

└── Responsible Gambling
```

---

## Step 4: Source Requirements

**TIER 1 (Primary) - MUST USE:**
- App Store: "4.9/5 (1.8M reviews) - analyzed [date range]"
- Google Play: "Reported in X reviews"
- Reddit: "Reddit users report [finding] (X threads)"

**TIER 2 (Verification):**
- Brand official sites for features only

**TIER 4 (Sparingly):**
- Affiliate sites for research gaps ONLY
- ❌ NEVER cite for pros/cons

---

## Step 5: Optimized FAQs

Create 7 FAQ questions targeting high-volume keywords:

**Example:**
1. "Is [Brand] legal in my state?" ← "is [brand] legal"
2. "Do I need a [Brand] promo code?" ← "[brand] promo code"
3. "How fast are [Brand] withdrawals?" ← "[brand] withdrawal time"
4. "Is [Brand] better than DraftKings?" ← "[brand] vs draftkings"
5. "What states is [Brand] available in?" ← "[brand] states"
6. "How do I download the [Brand] app?" ← "[brand] app download"
7. "What is the [Brand] welcome bonus?" ← "[brand] bonus"
