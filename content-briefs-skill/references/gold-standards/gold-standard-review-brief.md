## CONTENT OUTLINE - DETAILED

### H1: [Brand] Sportsbook Review: [Timeless Angle]
**[Last Updated Badge - Phase 3 adds]**

---

### Introduction (100-150 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- Verdict in first sentence (recommended/not recommended)
- Key strengths (2-3)
- Key weaknesses (1-2)
- Who this sportsbook is best for
- **NO affiliate disclosure**

---

### H2: [Brand] Overview (300-350 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- Company history and background
- Market positioning (premium, value, niche)
- Licensing and regulation
- Key statistics:
  - Years in operation
  - Markets served
  - User base (if available)

---

### H2: [Brand] Bonus: Current Welcome Offer (400-500 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] bonus" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- **Exact current offer**
- **Complete Terms & Conditions:**
  - Minimum deposit
  - Wagering requirements (explain what Xr means)
  - Minimum odds
  - Time limit
  - Excluded bet types
  - Promo code (if required)
- **Value assessment** - is this actually good?
- **Strategy tips** for meeting requirements

#### H3: Do I Need a [Brand] Promo Code? (100-150 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] promo code" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- Yes/No answer immediately
- Current promo code if applicable
- Where to enter code
- What happens if you forget

---

### H2: [Brand] App Review (500-600 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] app" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- **App Store rating:** "X.X/5 from X reviews"
- **Google Play rating:** "X.X/5 from X reviews"
- User interface assessment
- Navigation and bet placement
- Live betting on mobile
- Push notifications
- Unique mobile features
- Performance (speed, battery, crashes)
- iOS vs Android differences

#### H3: [Brand] App Download Guide (150-200 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] app download" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- iOS download steps
- Android download steps (APK if applicable)
- Verification requirements on mobile
- Storage requirements

---

### H2: [Signature Feature] (400-500 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- What makes [Brand] unique
- Deep dive on their best feature
- How it compares to competitors
- Real user feedback on this feature

---

### H2: Live Betting & Streaming (400-450 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- In-play market coverage
- Speed of odds updates
- Cash out functionality
- Live streaming availability
- Sports covered for streaming

---

### H2: Betting Markets (400-450 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- Sports offered
- Market depth per sport
- Odds quality (margins)
- Props and specials
- Market-specific coverage (e.g., GAA for Ireland)

---

### H2: Banking (400-450 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- **Deposit methods** with processing times
- **Withdrawal methods** with processing times
- Minimum/maximum limits
- Fees (if any)
- Verification requirements

#### H3: [Brand] Withdrawal Time (150-200 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] withdrawal" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- Method-by-method withdrawal times
- Fastest method recommendation
- Reddit user experiences on withdrawals

---

### H2: [Brand] vs [Competitor] (400-500 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] vs [competitor]" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- Head-to-head comparison table
- Winner by category (odds, bonus, app, markets)
- Who should choose [Brand]
- Who should choose [Competitor]

---

### H2: Is [Brand] Legal? (300-350 words)

**Target Keyword:** "is [brand] legal" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- Licensing information
- Regulatory bodies
- Player protection measures
- Security features

#### H3: Where is [Brand] Available? (150-200 words)

**Target Keyword:** "[brand] states/availability" (XXX/mo)

**Writer must cover:**
- List of available markets/states
- Restrictions or limitations
- Expansion plans (if known)

---

### Pros & Cons Summary (150-200 words)

**Writer must include:**
- **5-6 Pros with specific evidence**
- **3-4 Cons with specific evidence**
- Overall assessment

---

### FAQs (7-10 questions, 80-100 words each)

**All FAQs must target keywords from cluster.**

---

### Final Verdict (200-250 words)

**Writer must cover:**
- Clear recommendation (or not)
- Best for [type of bettor]
- Not ideal for [type of bettor]
- Summary of key strengths
- Call to action
