### Section 6: Bonus Types Explained (H2)
**Title:** `Types of [State] Sportsbook Bonuses`

```markdown
## Types of [State] Sportsbook Bonuses

Understanding bonus structures helps you choose the best [State] promo codes and offers:

### Deposit Match Bonuses
The sportsbook matches your first deposit up to a specified amount (e.g., 100% match up to $1,000). You receive bonus funds equal to your deposit that must be wagered before withdrawal.

**Example:** Deposit $500 ‚Üí Receive $500 in bonus funds ‚Üí Must wager $500 before withdrawing

### Risk-Free Bets (First Bet Insurance)
If your first bet loses, the sportsbook refunds your stake in bonus bets up to a maximum amount. If your bet wins, you keep winnings but don't receive bonus bets.

**Example:** Bet $1,000 first bet ‚Üí Loses ‚Üí Receive $1,000 in bonus bets

### Bet & Get Bonuses
Place a qualifying bet of a specified amount and receive bonus bets regardless of outcome. This is the most straightforward bonus type.

**Example:** Bet $5 ‚Üí Receive $150 in bonus bets (win or lose)

### Conditional Bonuses
Receive bonus bets only if your first bet wins. Lower risk for sportsbooks, but requires your qualifying bet to succeed.

**Example:** Bet $5 ‚Üí First bet wins ‚Üí Receive $150 in bonus bets

### Profit Boosts
Multiply your profit (not total return) on a winning bet. These are tokens applied to individual bets.

**Example:** $100 bet at +200 with 100% profit boost ‚Üí Win $400 instead of $300

### No Sweat Bets
Similar to risk-free bets but often structured as daily offers rather than one-time signup bonuses.

**Example:** $200 no-sweat bet daily for 10 days ‚Üí If any bet loses, refunded in bonus bets
```

### Can I claim multiple sportsbook bonuses in [State]?
Yes! Each sportsbook operates independently, so you can claim welcome bonuses from all licensed [State] operators. Most bettors sign up with 3-5 sportsbooks to maximize bonuses and compare odds.

### Do I need a promo code for every [State] sportsbook?
No. Only BetMGM, Caesars, bet365, and BetRivers require promo codes. FanDuel, DraftKings, and Fanatics automatically apply bonuses without codes.

### What happens if I forget to enter a promo code?
Contact customer support immediately before placing your first bet. Most sportsbooks can manually add promo codes within 24 hours of registration if you haven't wagered yet.

### Can I withdraw bonus bets?
No. Bonus bets must be wagered before withdrawal. When your bonus bet wins, you receive the profit (not the stake) as withdrawable cash.

### How long do [State] sportsbook bonuses last?
Most bonus bets expire 7 days after issuance. Profit boost tokens typically expire after 14 days. Check specific terms for each sportsbook.

### What are wagering requirements?
Most [State] sportsbooks require 1x wagering (bet the bonus amount once). This is industry-standard and means you can withdraw after placing one bet with your bonus.

### Do [State] bonuses require odds minimums?
Yes. Common minimums are -200 or longer (e.g., -150, +100, +200). This prevents betting heavily-favored outcomes with minimal risk.

### Can I use [State] sportsbook bonuses on any sport?
Generally yes, but some bonuses restrict certain markets (e.g., no props, no live betting). Check specific bonus terms before placing bets.

### Are [State] sportsbook promos available year-round?
Welcome bonuses are always available for new users. Seasonal promotions increase during NFL, NBA playoffs, and March Madness.

### Do I pay taxes on [State] sportsbook bonuses?
Bonus bets themselves aren't taxable. However, winnings from bonus bets are taxable income. Sportsbooks issue W-2G forms for wins of $600+ or 300x your stake.

### Section 10: Terms to Know (H2)
**Title:** `[State] Sportsbook Bonus Terms & Conditions`

```markdown
## [State] Sportsbook Bonus Terms & Conditions

Understanding key terms helps you avoid surprises when claiming bonuses:

### Bonus Bet vs. Cash
**Bonus bets** must be wagered and only the profit (not stake) is withdrawable. **Cash** can be withdrawn immediately without wagering.

### Odds Restrictions
Most bonuses require minimum odds (commonly -200 or longer) to prevent risk-free arbitrage betting.

### Playthrough Requirements
The number of times you must wager a bonus before withdrawing. [State] sportsbooks typically have 1x playthrough (very favorable).

### Expiration Dates
Bonus bets typically expire 7 days after issuance. Profit boost tokens expire after 14 days. Unused bonuses cannot be extended.

### One Per Household
Welcome bonuses are limited to one per person, household, IP address, and payment method. Using multiple accounts for bonuses violates terms of service.

### Eligible Markets
Some bonuses exclude certain bet types (e.g., live betting, props, futures). Check restrictions before placing bets.

### Maximum Stakes
Bonus bets often have maximum stake limits ($25-$50 per bet). This prevents users from placing a single large wager with bonus funds.

### Void Bets
If a bet is voided (e.g., game postponed), bonus bets are usually returned to your account with the same expiration date.
```
